About

Dr. Shruti Kolli is a dedicated Consultant Cardiologist with extensive experience in managing complex cardiac conditions in both adult and pediatric patients. Over the years, she has gained specialized training in advanced cardiology procedures, heart failure management, and non-invasive as well as interventional techniques.

  • Performing detailed adult and pediatric 2D echocardiography
  • Advanced ECG, Holter, HUTT, CPET analysis
  • Exposure to EP & RFA, 3D CARTO mapping and assisting structural heart procedures
  • Experience in advanced imaging (IVUS, OCT) and therapeutics (Rota ablation, IVL)
  • Management of heart failure, cardiomyopathy, ECMO, IMPELLA cases
  • Exposure to CRT, TAVI, leadless pacemakers (Micra), and cardiac transplants
